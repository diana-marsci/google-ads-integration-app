Google Ads Data Integration App

This application enables secure, OAuth-based access to Google Ads data for reporting and analysis.

Users can authenticate with their Google account, authorize access to their ad data, and view/export data to destinations like Google Sheets or Google Cloud Storage.

Our solution is built using Flask, Airbyte, and Google Ads API, with a strong focus on data security and transparency.
